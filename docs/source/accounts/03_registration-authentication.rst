*****************************
Registration & Authentication
*****************************

temi Mobile App
===============
- The temi Mobile app is central to temi's ecosystem.
  - It acts as a registration system for temi Robot 
  - It acts as an authentication mechanism for temi Center 
- When installing for the first time, you will be asked for your mobile's phone number, name, and email address
- Your mobile phone number will act as a unique identifier for your account 
- Name can be changed after registration, but email cannot. Registering again with a different email will causes errors.


temi Robot
==========
- When turning on temi Robot for the first time, you will need to register the robot using the mobile app


temi Center
===========
- When accessing temi Center, authentication is accomplished using the temi Mobile App
